TAMIL NADU

  • Tamil Nadu is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and classical arts, including Bharatanatyam dance and Carnatic music

  • Home to numerous ancient temples like Meenakshi Amman Temple, Brihadeeswara Temple, and Rameswaram Temple, showcasing Dravidian architecture

  • Diverse landscapes ranging from the Nilgiri Hills in the Western Ghats to the coastal plains along the Bay of Bengal.

  • Famous for vibrant festivals such as Pongal, a harvest festival, and the elaborate celebrations of Tamil New Year.

  • Known for its distinct and flavorful cuisine, including dishes like dosa, idli, sambar, and various types of biryanis.

  • Chennai, the capital city, is a major economic hub in South India, with thriving industries like automobile, IT, and textiles
