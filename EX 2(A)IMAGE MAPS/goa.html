GOA

  • Goa is situated on the southwestern coast of India, bordered by the Arabian Sea, known for its picturesque beaches and vibrant coastal lifestyle.

  • The capital of Goa is Panaji (Panjim), a charming city with Portuguese colonial architecture, vibrant markets, and a lively cultural scene.

  • Covering an area of approximately 3,702 square kilometers, Goa is a relatively small state but rich in natural beauty and diverse attractions.


  • Goa experiences a tropical climate with hot, humid summers, a monsoon season from June to September, and a pleasant, cooler winter from November to March.

  • Popular Beaches: Famous beaches include Baga, known for its nightlife; Anjuna, popular for its parties; Calangute, a family-friendly destination; and Palolem, known for its serene beauty.
