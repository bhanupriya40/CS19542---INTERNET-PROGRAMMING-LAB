Administration Services

The Administrative Department of the college is headed by the Administrative Officer. The Administrative Office serves as the backbone of the Institution by handling Student Service, HR, Purchase, Dispatch, and all official correspondences with the regulatory bodies such as AICTE, DTE, Anna University, etc. The department is well-computerized with an exclusive file management system for staff and students.

Students may contact the office for:
  • Identity card
  • Bonafide certificate
  • Bus pass
  • Fee related queries
  • Hall ticket
  • Medical emergencies
  • Any other grievances

Library

The College library is located in a spacious building of 9000 sq. ft. The library is open to students and staff members from 7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. on all working days. It has a collection of around 52,600 volumes of textbooks, reference books, national and international journals in all branches of Engineering, Science, Technology, Management, and General Studies. The books are classified according to the Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme, and the library follows an open access system.

Canteen

The college is equipped with a fully functional vegetarian canteen that serves hygienically prepared food to the staff and students. A subsidized mess is also available, with a separate mess for boys.

Hostel

We have separate hostels for boys and girls. The hostel can accommodate 500 boys and 200 girls. Final year and PG students are provided single rooms and are permitted to use their own computers. The mess includes a spacious dining hall and kitchen, with separate seating arrangements for boys and girls.

Rules and Regulations:
  • Students must occupy the rooms allotted to them by the Warden (Principal).
  • Refrain from anti-social and undesirable activities such as consumption of alcohol, tobacco, gambling, ragging, etc.
  • Students are responsible for the cleanliness of their rooms.
  • No student will be allowed to leave the hostel based on a phone call.
  • Parents are requested to provide a list of authorized visitors/local guardians. Visitors not listed will not be permitted.
  • Students may go home only if the college is closed continuously for five days or more.
  • Students wishing to attend family events will need parental approval through the Principal.
  • Visitors are allowed on holidays from 11:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M.

Transport

The college has a full-fledged Transport Department with 119 buses to provide safe transportation for students and staff from various locations. Additional buses are added based on the student intake.
