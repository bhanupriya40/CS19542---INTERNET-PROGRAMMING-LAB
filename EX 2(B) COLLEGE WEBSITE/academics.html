Academics

Overview

Our college is dedicated to delivering a high-quality education that promotes intellectual growth and professional development. Our academic programs are designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields. We offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines, all supported by a committed faculty and state-of-the-art facilities.

Undergraduate Programs

Our undergraduate programs are tailored to give students a strong foundation in their chosen fields. We offer the following programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (B.Sc. IT): Focuses on software development, computer systems, and data science. Topics include programming, algorithms, databases, and network security.
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Engineering (B.Sc. CSE): Covers computer systems, software engineering, and artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on developing practical solutions to complex problems.
  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (B.Sc. Mech Engg): Offers training in mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing processes, including robotics, fluid dynamics, and mechanical design.
  • Bachelor of Science in Food Technology (B.Sc. Food Tech): Provides education in food processing, preservation, and safety. Students learn about food chemistry, microbiology, and quality control.

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs are designed for those seeking advanced knowledge and research opportunities. We offer the following programs:

  • Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc. IT): Focuses on advanced topics such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity, including research components and project work.
  • Master of Science in Computer Science Engineering (M.Sc. CSE): Specializes in advanced computer systems, software engineering, and data science, with detailed study and experimentation.
  • Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering (M.Sc. Mech Engg): Provides in-depth knowledge in advanced mechanical systems, robotics, and thermal systems, featuring project-based learning and research opportunities.
  • Master of Science in Food Technology (M.Sc. Food Tech): Emphasizes advanced food processing, safety, and quality control, combining coursework with research projects.
